# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Kenyan consumers shopping for high-quality electronics, fashion, and home goods. They value clear pricing in KES, instant and secure mobile payment channels (M-Pesa STK & Till), and reliable delivery details.

## Product Purpose
Biashara Hub serves as a high-performance, open-source headless e-commerce store custom-built for the Kenyan market. It aims to eliminate heavy third-party platform fees by providing full data ownership and production-grade reliability.

## Brand Personality
Editorial, premium, structured, sophisticated. It uses a high-fashion editorial visual grammar, combining spacious layouts with larger serif display headers and a clean, high-contrast grid system to evoke high trust.

## Anti-references
- SaaS-cream/beige background templates (avoid the saturated warm-beige AI default).
- Over-rounded card grids (caps cards at 12–16px border-radius, no over-rounding).
- Cluttered, generic marketplace interfaces with heavy shadows, diagonal repeating stripes, and tiny uppercase kick eyebrows on every section.
- Low-contrast light-gray text on tinted background.

## Design Principles
1. **Editorial Restraint**: Prioritize spacious typography and thin, precise grid-light borders rather than card wrappers and heavy drop-shadows.
2. **Kenyan-First Trust**: Local payment methods (STK push, Till C2B validation) and flat shipping rates must feel like first-class, seamless citizens.
3. **Structured Hierarchy**: Utilize clear typographical scale contrast (large serif display headers, clean sans-serif body text) with a strict color balance (70% background whites/slate-50, 20% primary navy text, 10% minimal gold highlights).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios must strictly hit WCAG AA (>= 4.5:1 for body and placeholder texts).
- Touch target sizes must remain >= 44px on mobile viewports (320px/390px widths).
- Full reduced-motion alternative transitions (@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)) for all entrance overlays.
